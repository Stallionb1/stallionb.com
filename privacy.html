Privacy Policy

At Stallion.B, we are committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your data when you visit our website, make a purchase, or interact with us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping address, and any details you enter when placing an order.
  • Order Information: Items purchased, payment details (handled securely by trusted payment providers), and transaction confirmations.
  • Technical & Usage Data: IP address, device type, browser details, pages visited, time spent on pages, and actions such as clicks.
  • Voluntary Data: Messages, feedback, or inquiries submitted via forms or email.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To provide customer service and respond to your questions.
  • To personalize your shopping experience.
  • To improve our website performance and user experience.
  • To send order updates and (if subscribed) promotional messages.
  • To maintain security, detect fraud, and prevent misuse.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to remember your preferences, keep your items saved in cart, analyze website performance, and make browsing faster. You may modify cookie settings in your browser, but some features may not work correctly without them.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or trade your personal data. We only share your information with:

  • Trusted payment processors (e.g., Visa, JazzCash).
  • Courier and delivery partners for order shipping.
  • Service providers for analytics, security, and hosting.

All partners are required to keep your data confidential and use it only for the intended purpose.

5. Data Protection & Security

We use secure encryption, restricted access, and monitoring systems to safeguard your information. While no system can guarantee absolute security, we continually update our protections.

6. Your Rights

  • Request access to the information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Request deletion of your data (where legally allowed).
  • Opt-out of promotional emails at any time.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary—typically for order completion, record‑keeping, legal compliance, or to provide customer support.

8. Children’s Privacy

Stallion.B does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If such data is detected, we will remove it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.

10. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, email us at info.stallionb@gmail.com.
